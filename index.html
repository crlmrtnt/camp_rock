Camp Rock festival program

The Camp Rock festival is a festival of current music, which
takes place on the last weekend of July: July 30, 31 and August 1.
The festival was created in 1989 and takes place since the
2015 edition over 5 days with 7 stages that are located throughout
the site. The capacity of the site is 45 000 people per day and
35 000 people for the camping.
